# Erda City Zoning — Quick Reference

> **Status: stub.** Erda incorporated relatively recently (2022) and is
> still standing up its land-use code. Verify everything against the live
> ordinance before relying on this. Also note: there is an active
> disconnection effort underway involving 7,000+ acres — confirm the
> parcel is still inside Erda's boundary at the time of analysis.

## Districts (placeholder — verify against current ordinance)

Erda has been working from a base that mirrored Tooele County's pre-
incorporation zoning, with revisions toward larger minimum lot sizes to
preserve rural character. Common designations include rural residential
with multi-acre minimums.

## Active issues to know

- **Disconnection petitions**: as of 2026, multiple landowners have filed
  to leave Erda and revert to unincorporated county. If the subject
  parcel is one of them, the relevant entitlement authority may shift
  mid-process.
- **General Plan**: Erda's general plan is comparatively new and is the
  controlling document for future land use designations. Check the most
  recent adopted version.
