# Wasatch Front / Tooele Valley Land Use Digest
**Period: April 13–28, 2026 (with select forward items through May 14)**

## This Week's Signal
Grantsville is the story. Two density-up rezones moved into the Planning Commission pipeline within two weeks — Wellstone (R-1-21 → R-1-12 near Pear/Quirk) on April 21 and Orion Subdivision (RR-1/RM-7 → RM-15 at 800 East Main) on May 5 — alongside an updated Annexation Policy Plan (Ord. 2026-16) adopted April 15 and Erda simultaneously processing two disconnection petitions on May 14. The boundary and density framework on the west side of the valley is being rewritten in real time.

## What Moved
- **Grantsville Annexation Policy Plan** (Ord. 2026-16, adopted Apr 15) — sets the rules for what Grantsville will pull in next. Read this before underwriting anything in the unincorporated donut.
- **Wellstone Subdivision** (Grantsville, Apr 21) — General Plan amendment + rezone from R-1-21 to R-1-12 across four parcels near Pear and Quirk. Roughly doubles allowed density on the parcels in question.
- **Orion Subdivision** (Grantsville, May 5) — Rezone from RR-1/RM-7 to **RM-15** at 800 East Main, four parcels. RM-15 is the highest-density residential category in play locally; this is the most aggressive ask in the region this period.
- **Sun Sage Terrace Phases 4–6** (Grantsville, Apr 15) — Concept plan presentation. Pipeline continuation, but worth tracking for absorption signals on the east end of town.
- **Copper Cove Phase 1** (Erda Planning, Apr 15 and Apr 28) — 45-lot preliminary plat appeared on consecutive PC agendas, suggesting it didn't clear cleanly the first time. Worth reading the minutes.
- **Erda Disconnection Petitions** (Erda CC, May 14) — Two parcels petitioning *out* of Erda city limits. First disconnection activity since incorporation that I've flagged. Identity of petitioners and parcel locations matter.
- **Le Property concept** (Grantsville PC, May 5) — Mixed Use concept at 454 East Main. Main Street MU activity is thin; any concept-stage MU on Main is worth a call.
- **519 W Main RV storage CUP** (Grantsville PC, Apr 21) — Outdoor RV storage CUP on Main Street frontage. Low-impact use locking up a Main Street parcel is a holding-pattern signal.

## Pattern Watch
- **Grantsville is rezoning up, not out (yet).** Both Wellstone and Orion are infill density increases, not greenfield annexations. Combined with the new Annexation Policy Plan, the city looks like it's trying to absorb growth inside existing boundaries before extending the line. If you're holding unincorporated dirt expecting a pull-in, the timeline may be longer than you think.
- **East Main Street corridor is heating up.** Orion (800 E Main, RM-15 ask), Le Property (454 E Main, MU concept), and Sun Sage Terrace are all clustered on Grantsville's east side. Three concept/rezone touches on the same corridor in 14 days isn't coincidence.
- **Erda is showing stress at the edges.** Copper Cove's repeat PC appearance plus two disconnection petitions in the same month suggests friction between the city's review posture and landowner expectations. Worth watching whether disconnections get granted — that sets precedent.
